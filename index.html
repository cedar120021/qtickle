About Me:

I used to suffer from persistent nail biting and picking, which led to damaged, painful nails and a constant feeling of insecurity. While acrylics and gel offered a temporary fix, they ultimately weakened my nails and were costly. This experience motivated me do thorough research about the benefits of various oils and the effect they have on cuticles and nails. I eventually formulated and tested several oil blends, and finally settled on a final formula that showed significant improvement in my nail health within the first two weeks of use. From that point, I began to take my nail care more seriously. I realized many others faced similar issues: thin, brittle, cracking, bendy, stunted, short nails.

I decided to create 10-20 nail pens to give away to friends and family as a way to test the oil on many nail types. The result was AMAZING. Recognizing that many people shared similar nail concerns, I decided to expand my efforts and offer this transformative product to the public.

Each bottle of Qtickle nail oil contains a mix of natural oil blend: Ingredients, Argan Oil, Castor Oil, Sweet Almond Oil, Coconut Oil, Golden Jojoba Oil, Sunflower Seed Oil, and Rosemary Oil.
